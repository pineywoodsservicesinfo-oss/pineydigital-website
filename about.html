About

Building for East Texas businesses

Not a big agency. Not a freelancer who disappears. A local developer who builds real systems that help real businesses grow.

How I ended up here

I moved to East Texas over 10 years ago and basically grew up here. My wife and I met here, and both of our kids were born here. This is home — not just where I work, but where my family lives and where I'm invested in the community.

Before Piney Digital, I worked a lot of different jobs. I was a server — that's where I really connected with people in this town and understood what local business looks like from the inside. I've done welding, framing, and right now I'm working in landscaping. Every one of those jobs taught me something about what small businesses need and how they operate.

How I got into development

I started learning development through online courses and self-study. As technology and AI tools exploded, I saw something clear: the tools are getting easier, but the work behind them hasn't gone away.

Anyone can throw together a website in a few hours now. That part is easy. But the backend work, the refinement, the SEO that actually works, the systems that bring customers back — that's what most people aren't ready for. That's where I help.

Why Piney Digital exists

Too many East Texas businesses have websites that don't work for them. Either they paid someone who disappeared after launch, or they're stuck with a site that's slow, outdated, and invisible on Google.

I built Piney Digital to solve that. Not just by making websites — but by building complete digital systems that bring customers back, generate reviews, and grow with your business.

My background matters here. I've worked in the trenches of local business. I know what it's like to deal with customers face-to-face, to worry about payroll, to need more leads next week. That's not abstract to me — that's real.

What makes this different

Most web designers deliver a site and walk away. You pay once, you get a finished product, and then you're on your own. Every update costs extra. Every change requires hourly rates. And if you want something more — like a loyalty program or automated text messages — that's a separate project.

I do it differently. Monthly plans mean I stay invested. Your site stays updated. Your tools keep working. And when something breaks or you need a change, you don't have to negotiate a new contract or hunt down a developer — you just message me.

That's why the loyalty system, SMS marketing, and review automation are built in. These aren't add-ons I'm trying to upsell — they're core to what makes the difference between a website and a growth system.

Why I focus on East Texas

This is my community. When you call, I answer — not a call center, not an overseas team. I understand what it means to serve local customers, and I know that word of mouth matters more in communities like ours.

That's why I don't just build websites and leave. I build relationships. Your success is my portfolio. Your referrals are my marketing. If you grow, I grow.
