💡 Why monthly instead of one-time?

Most web designers build a site and walk away. Then you're stuck paying hourly rates for every little update, or worse — your site goes stale and stops working.

With a monthly plan, you get:

  • A website that stays current and secure
  • Regular updates without hourly charges
  • Real tools that bring customers back (loyalty, SMS, reviews)
  • Someone invested in your long-term growth

For less than $10/day on the Growth plan, you're not just buying a website — you're getting a digital growth partner who keeps the system running while you focus on your business.
